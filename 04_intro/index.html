### Week 4: Conflicting Variables and Resolution **Conflicting** * Cost versus convenience * Multiple smaller bricks means easier transport, but more steel needed so more expensive * More complex cart means more convenience and easier for users to lift bricks but also more cost * Larger salt brick means longer heating but is more difficult to transport * More complex brick shape means easier carrying but is more difficult to manufacture and thus more expensive **Things for Thought** * The salt brick is about 20 pounds, so if children are to transport it, much of the lifting will need to be provided through mechanical advantage from the cart * Insulation is needed on the individual brick throughout the entire transportation process * Properties * withstand the 600C * have the mechanical strength to be able to hold when the salt brick is being lifted * Form insulation comes in - sheets, batts, formed/molded shapes? * Going around the brick - detachable panels, box with cover, bag? * Manufacturing - locations and methods of connecting seams and edges * Can we reduce the cost of the multiple smaller bricks if we used a different metal? * Getting the cart over the threshold * modification to the wheels * cart tips or rotates over threshold
